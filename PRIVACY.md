# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 9, 2025**

## Introduction

Profit AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Shopify application.

By installing and using Profit AI, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Our services are designed for businesses and are not intended for personal or household use.

---

## Information We Collect

When you install and use Profit AI, we collect the following types of information:

### Store Information
- Your Shopify store domain and basic store details necessary to provide our services

### Order Data
- Order information (totals, dates, customer types) to calculate profit metrics
- We do **not** store individual customer personal information

### Cost Data
- Fixed costs, marketing costs, and other financial data that you input into the app

### Integration Data
- If you connect advertising platforms (Facebook Ads, Google Ads), we store connection credentials and retrieve ad spend data

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Calculate and display profit metrics and analytics
- Generate profit projections and planning tools
- Track marketing costs and return on ad spend
- Provide unit economics analysis
- Improve and optimize our services

---

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. We use PostgreSQL databases hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (Railway). Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

We retain your data for as long as your app installation is active. Upon uninstallation, your data is automatically deleted within 48 hours in compliance with Shopify's requirements.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties.

We may share data only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** We may share data with trusted third-party services that help us operate our app (e.g., cloud hosting providers)
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights

## Your Privacy Rights

### For EEA Residents (GDPR)

You have the right to:
- **Access** – Request copies of your personal data
- **Rectification** – Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** – Request deletion of your data
- **Data Portability** – Request transfer of your data

### For California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- Request deletion of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.
